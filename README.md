Investment logic:

Speculative buying and flipping (“炒货”) has a significant marginal impact on storage prices.

Storage price rallies are closely tied to supply shortages, not FOMO. 
When shortage starts to ease, a massive amount of supply (selling pressure / chips) floods the market. 
Company fundamentals remain solid, everything has well been priced with pre-calculations. 
Holding base is extremely strong, but also could be a potential risk — once shortages start to loosen up then tend to sell en masse

“Flash Card” is a NAND price reference.
Flash cards (MicroSD, TF cards, USB flash drives, etc.) are fundamentally built around NAND Flash particles at their core.
The Flash Market divides NAND into several major categories, among which “Flash Card” specifically provides the dedicated spot-market quotations for NAND in Huaqiangbei’s channel market and industry market.

存储涨跟“痛”很相关，不是fomo，比fomo更可怕的被裹挟买

之后痛感产生故事定价，定价被伴随毛利率、收益证明形成循环

“闪存卡”是 NAND 的参考
闪存卡（MicroSD、TF 卡、USB 闪存盘等）核心就是 NAND Flash 颗粒。
闪存市场把 NAND 分成好几个大类，其中闪存卡就是专门给华强北渠道/行业市场的 NAND 现货报价
